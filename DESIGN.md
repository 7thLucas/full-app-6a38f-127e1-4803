## Design Direction: IntakeFlow Landing Page

### Feeling
Calm, professional, trustworthy, and modern — reassuring rather than clinical-cold. Mobile-first, generous whitespace, soft and breathable. A patient of any age should feel instantly at ease and know exactly what to do.

### Palette (soft blue/teal)
- **Primary:** teal — base around teal-600/teal-700 (e.g. #0d9488 / #0f766e) for the primary CTA, brand accent dot, and key highlights.
- **Soft accents:** teal-50 / teal-100 tints for icon chips, subtle backgrounds, and reassurance cards.
- **Neutrals (text & structure):** slate — slate-900 for headings, slate-600 for body, slate-400 for muted labels, slate-200 for hairline borders.
- **Surface:** clean white / very soft off-white background. Optional gentle teal-tinted gradient or soft radial wash behind the hero for warmth — kept subtle.
- High contrast between text and background for accessibility.

### Typography
- Clean, legible, modern sans-serif (system/Inter-style). Patients of all ages — prioritize legibility over personality.
- Clear hierarchy: a large but gentle hero headline (not shouty), comfortable body size (never below ~16px on mobile), and small uppercase tracked labels (tracking ~0.2em, teal-700) for section eyebrows — matching the established blueprint style.
- Generous line-height for body copy.

### Forms & shapes
- Clean, rounded, modern forms: rounded-2xl cards (border border-slate-200), soft rounded-full or rounded-xl buttons.
- The primary CTA ("Begin intake") is the single boldest element: solid teal fill, white text, comfortable tap target (large, full-width on mobile, generous padding), gentle hover/press states, clearly the one thing to do.
- Soft, restrained elevation — prefer subtle shadows / hairline borders over heavy drop shadows. Rounded icon chips (rounded-xl, teal-50 fill) for any "what to expect" points.

### Layout
- **Mobile-first**, single-column, vertically rhythmic with generous spacing.
- Hero: brand mark + tagline, welcoming headline, short reassurance line, then the prominent "Begin intake" CTA high on the page.
- "What to expect" section: a small set of short, scannable reassurance points (icon + short label + one line each), in calm cards.
- Quiet footer with a privacy-respecting note.
- Scales gracefully to larger screens (centered, comfortable max-width container) but the phone experience is the priority.

### Accessibility
- Legible type sizes for older patients, strong color contrast, large tap targets, clear focus states on the CTA, semantic structure.

### Consistency with existing brand blueprints
- Match the established blueprint visual language: teal-700 eyebrow labels (uppercase, tracked), slate-900 headings, slate-600 body, rounded-2xl bordered cards, teal-50 icon chips. The landing page should feel like the same product family as the existing IntakeFlow blueprints.
