The Foundation

The Problem

Small clinics run their front desk on paper and phone tag. Patients fill clipboards on arrival, staff re-key the same details, and confirmation calls eat hours every week — while the waiting room backs up and no-shows slip through.

The Solution

One shared flow. Patients submit intake from their own phone before they arrive, staff work a single live queue instead of a paper stack, and every submission fires an automatic SMS confirmation — closing the clipboard-and-callback loop.
